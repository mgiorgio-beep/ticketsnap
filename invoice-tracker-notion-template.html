📦 What's Included

Invoice Database — log every invoice with line items
Each invoice is a Notion page. Log vendor, amount, date, due date, location, and individual line items. Status auto-updates based on due date and payment status.
AP Dashboard — total outstanding, aging, and due-this-week
Summary cards and aging breakdown update automatically as you log invoices and payments. See exactly what's owed, what's due soon, and what's overdue.
Vendor Setup — terms, payment methods, contacts
Store each vendor's payment terms (Net 14, Net 30, COD), preferred payment method, remittance address, and contact info. Linked to invoices so you always know the terms.
Payment Log — track every payment with method and check #
Record payments by date, amount, method (check, ACH, cash, Venmo), and which invoice(s) it covers. Invoice balances auto-update.
Multi-Location Support — filter everything by site
Each invoice tagged with location. Filter the entire dashboard by site to see AP for one location or all locations combined.
