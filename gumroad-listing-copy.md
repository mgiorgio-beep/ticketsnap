## Product Description

### The Problem

You're running a restaurant. You know food costs matter — but between managing staff, dealing with vendors, and keeping the kitchen running, who has time to sit down and calculate the cost of every dish?

So you guess. You eyeball it. And every month, your food cost report comes back higher than it should be.

### The Fix

This Notion template does the math for you. Enter your ingredients, plug in vendor prices, build your recipes, and instantly see your plate cost, food cost %, and profit per dish — all in one clean dashboard.

When a vendor raises prices (and they will), update it once and every affected recipe recalculates automatically.

**Built by a restaurant owner who got tired of spreadsheet chaos.**

## What's Inside

**Master Ingredient Database**
- Track every ingredient you buy: vendor, pack size, pack price, unit cost
- Unit cost auto-calculates — no manual math
- Filter by category (Produce, Seafood, Dairy, Dry Goods, etc.)
- Log price changes with dates so you can see trends

**Recipe Builder**
- Add ingredients from your master list with quantities used
- Line cost, plate cost, and food cost % calculate instantly
- Supports batch recipes (make 10 servings, see cost per serving)
- Duplicate the blank template for each new recipe in under 30 seconds

**Menu Costing Dashboard**
- Every recipe in one sortable table
- See food cost %, plate cost, menu price, and gross profit at a glance
- Color-coded status flags: Under Target / On Target / OVER TARGET
- Summary stats: average food cost %, recipes over threshold, avg profit per plate

**Vendor Price Tracker**
- Log price changes over time
- See which products are trending up
- Know how many recipes are affected by each price change

**Bonus: Excel Companion Spreadsheet**
- Full Excel version with 113 working formulas
- CSV import template to bulk-load your ingredients
- Use alongside Notion or as a standalone tool

## How to Get Started

1. Duplicate the template into your Notion workspace (free Notion plan works)
2. Open your latest vendor invoices
3. Add your top 10 ingredients with prices and pack sizes
4. Build your first recipe — plate cost and food cost % appear instantly
5. Add more recipes as you go — the dashboard updates automatically

Total setup time: ~15 minutes for your first 5 recipes.

## FAQ

**Do I need Notion paid plan?**
No. This works on the free Notion plan.

**Can I add my own recipes?**
Yes — unlimited. Duplicate the blank recipe template for each new dish.

**What if I have multiple locations?**
Add a "Location" property to filter recipes and ingredients by site.

**Does it work for bar/cocktail costing?**
Yes. Works for any recipe — food, cocktails, coffee drinks, anything with ingredients and a selling price.

**Can I import my existing ingredient list?**
Yes — the included CSV template lets you bulk-import ingredients instead of typing them one by one.

**What's the food cost % target?**
Industry standard is 28-35% for food, 18-22% for bar. The dashboard flags anything over your chosen threshold.
