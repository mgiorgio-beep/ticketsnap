📐 Formulas Used (auto-calculated)
Unit Cost = Pack Price ÷ Pack Size
Line Cost = Unit Cost × Qty Used
Plate Cost = SUM(all Line Costs)
Food Cost % = (Plate Cost ÷ Menu Price) × 100
Gross Profit = Menu Price − Plate Cost

📦 What's Included in This Template

Recipe Database — add unlimited recipes with ingredients
Each recipe is a Notion page with a linked ingredient database. Fill in your vendor pack sizes and prices, and the formulas handle the rest. Categories, tags, and status let you filter and organize by section.
Ingredient & Vendor Database — track pack sizes, prices, vendors
Master list of every ingredient you buy. Link to one or more vendors, log pack sizes, and the unit cost is auto-calculated. When a price changes, every recipe using that ingredient updates automatically.
Menu Overview Dashboard — sort all dishes by food cost %
One table with every menu item, its plate cost, food cost %, and profit. Sort by food cost % to instantly find your problem dishes. Color-coded status tags flag items that are over your target threshold.
Price Change Log — flag vendor price increases
Every time you update a vendor price, the old price is logged with the date. See which products are trending up and how many recipes are affected.
Category Targets — set food cost % goals per menu section
Set target food cost percentages for each category (appetizers, entrées, bar, desserts). The dashboard highlights any dish that exceeds its category target so you can adjust portions, pricing, or sourcing.
Quick-Start Guide — get set up in 15 minutes
Step-by-step walkthrough: (1) Add your top 10 dishes, (2) Enter ingredient costs from your latest invoices, (3) Set your menu prices, (4) Review your food cost dashboard. You'll have actionable data in under 15 minutes.
